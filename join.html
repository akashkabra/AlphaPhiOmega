Thinking About Joining Alpha Phi Omega?

Alpha Phi Omega teaches through the principles of Leadership, Friendship and Service that we are architects of our own ambitions and that each of us has the opportunity to develop ourselves to be whatever we seek to be.

Requirements To Join (RUSH FALL 2019)

New Member Requirements

  • Attend four (4) rush events for at least one (1) hour each.
  • Attend at least one (1) of each event: Leadership/Informational (I), Service (S), and Fellowship (F).
  • Complete five (5) brother interviews by the last day of rush week.
  • Have a minimum cumulative GPA of 2.5.

PNM Academic/GPA Requirements

  • Completed one full semester at Rutgers University
  • Earned a minimum of 12 credits
  • Must have a minimum cumulative GPA of 2.5

New Member Fees

  • National Dues: $32.00
  • National Initiation Fee: $35.00
  • Brotherbood Fees: $36.00
  • Other: $17.00

  • TOTAL DUES: $120.00

  • After pledging, active brothers dues are $85.00 every semester

Membership Expectations

  • Complete minimum of 24 hours of service
  • Must have a certain amout of hours in each category
